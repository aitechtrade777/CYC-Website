Our Journey

Building the Foundations for Your Success

Established in 2024, CYC ECO SDN BHD is a dynamic trading and consultancy firm committed to delivering high-quality industrial materials and transformative business solutions.

We began our journey with a focus on sustainability, curating a range of eco-friendly consumer products—including bamboo body brushes, natural cleaners, and organic cotton makeup accessories—that reflect our dedication to quality and ethical sourcing.

Today, we have evolved into a leading specialist in the Plywood Trading sector, supplying premium-grade timber products to meet the rigorous demands of the modern construction and interior industries.
